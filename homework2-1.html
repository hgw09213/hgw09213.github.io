Types of RPGs

  • Tabletop RPGs: Players use a board, dice, and models to play in a fictional world.
  • Live action RPGs: Players physically perform their characters' actions.
  • Multi-User Dungeons (MUDs): Players interact in a text-based world.
  • Massively multiplayer online role-playing games (MMORPGs): Players interact in a graphics-based world.
